{% extends "base_app.html" %} {% comment %} The retrospective's record (#25): one page that tells the whole story and tells the same story to every reader. Nothing on it varies with who is reading it. `_docs/decisions.md` item 10 governs the card list below absolutely. A card is its category and its text and nothing else — every card renders identically to every other. Where a "who wrote it" or an "Anonymous" label might sit there is nothing at all. No author, no anonymity flag, no anonymity count, no card handle (`pk` or `public_id`) is rendered, and the cards come from `revealed_cards()`, so their order is `position` and never submission order. The card list is the one `#feedback-cards` container; the notes, participation, decisions and action items render outside it, so the display names the page legitimately carries — note authors, action owners, the participation list — never appear beside a card. The viewer's own cards are not marked here, unlike the board. A server-rendered read view, not the React island: it renders no control that mutates anything, so opening it changes nothing and it looks the same at every refresh for every member. {% endcomment %} {% block title %}Retrospective summary — week of {{ cycle.week_start|date:'j F Y' }}{% endblock %} {% block content %}
